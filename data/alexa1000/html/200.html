The Beast Race is a 10k obstacle...... Read More

The Beast Race is a 10k obstacle race over rough terrain, dirt tracks and forest in Scotland, and the free website which promotes the event is informative without compromising on aesthetics. In the homepage, navigation links are set in a full-width toolbar along the top (each tab highlighted in orange turn, so visitors always know where they are) and social media buttons are placed on the top left a familiar location for most website visitors. Three image teasers, two dedicated to different races and one to obstacles, invite visitors to click them to find out more about each. Each race- the Loch Ness Race and the Banchory Race is awarded its own page, where the textual monotony is broken by images, and a smart visual hierarchy is used so visitors can scan the text quickly and easily. In the Obstacles page, all the obstacles are featured in an easy-to-update, thumbnail grid-styled portfolio with linkable, enlarged images on hover. Each page on the website, whether it's Teams or Prizes, has call-to-action buttons which direct visitors to follow up on their reading. The FAQ's page is categorized so visitors can check out the relevant information of their choosing. The Contact page features a customized contact widget so visitors can contact the organizers with any question they may have.
